college hill

Statement of Intent

year = 2014

label = UK Trends

genres = (Grime/150BPM/Bass/Instrumental)
College Hill takes bass pulses, harsh percussion and road vocal snippets and reworks them at 150bpm. The effect is of music that blends Juke & Grime into aggressive, urgent, rhythmically complex beats. Released in 2014 on Threnody's UK Trends label, remastered in 2020.
